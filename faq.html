How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

There are printing and copying machines at various points, managed by Oc√© Repro. The pantry also has a print / copy facility on each floor, which can be used with the HZ pass. On the ground floor there is also a repro room where the plotter is located.
As a student and employee, you can contact the Canon Reproshop on Edisonweg for large print jobs and for binding reports. You send your order by e-mail to Canon and within 24 hours (on working days) you have your print order returned. You can pick it up at the reception.

How can you scan a document a send it to your laptop at HZ?

-Ensure you hace at least 0.07 euro credit. Although scanning is free, you must have this amount of credit on you HZ pass.
-Register using your HZ pass on the TouchID next to the multifunctional printer.
-In the TouchID menu, select option Scanning - Scann.
-Lay the original sheet on the feeder or glass plate.

What do you need to do when you are sick / show symptoms of coronavirus?

Do you have serious complaints such as a cold, cough and / or fever? Stay at home and report sick. If the complaints get worse, contact your doctor or the GGD by telephone.

Report it immediately to your training coordinator or manager if you are suspected of being infected with the corona virus and also report sick. Are you an international student? Please also inform the International Office by e-mail at internationaloffice@hz.nl

How can you book a project space in one of the wings?

A total of 26 project group rooms can be found on the 1st, 2nd and 3rd floor. These can be reserved by employees and students themselves via the Self-service portal. The rooms are suitable for 6-8 people. They are equipped with an LCD screen, cables for connecting the laptop to the screen, power supplies in the wall and a whiteboard. View the reservation manual for the project group rooms.

What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

Employees and students can park their own car in the parking garage at Kousteensedijk 3 (between the Court and the Library). On presentation of the entry ticket, you will receive an exit ticket free of charge at the HZ reception. This is a pilot that will be evaluated by the municipality and HZ after one year.
