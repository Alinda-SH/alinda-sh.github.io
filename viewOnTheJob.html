View On the Job

Profession

NS

NS, Nederlandse Spoorwegen, (Dutch railways), is a Dutch company which owns and governs the railways and trains. The company has a informal work culture. They have as main focus their customers, the travelers. They are a big company spread through the Netherlands.

IT Roles

Data engineer
The position Data engineer plays an important role in getting and keeping data pipelines available at the NS. As a Data engineer, their role is to ensure that this data is reliable, but also processed in an efficiency and scalable manner. In addition, they ensure that this is done in a way that meets the requirements with regard to security and privacy.

SCRUM master
Scrum Master is an expert in the Agile philosophy and the Scrum framework and they ensure that both the teams and the environment of the teams are brought to a higher maturity level. They coach the team, the Product Owner and the organization. They ensure that the cross functional teams continue to improve and become more effective. They work together with stakeholders, development teams and other Scrum Masters. Listening well, communicating clearly and organizational sensitivity are indispensable here.

Security tester
Security testers within NS start with Threat modeling. In this way, the team identifies the security risks on which the security tests are based. With their knowledge and skills they participate as an expert in this, or they facilitate by taking control. With thorough threat modeling they create transparency and trust. Then they get to work with the design, execution and reporting of the security tests.

Senior fullstack developer
The Senior fullstack developer may well play the leading role in the translation of our strategy into products and services for our customers. They participate in challenging functionalities with the latest technologies. They also play an important role in our evolution towards a DevOps organization. They learn quickly and happily, they see where opportunities for improvement lie and they take others along. Important elements in Agile working such as collaboration, responding to changes and short-cyclical value delivery suit their way of working.

DevOps engineer.
They contribute to the design of the platform Stationsdata.nl, the place where all information about the station comes together in one place. Maps can be used to consult, manage and analyze information in order to simplify asset management and make all stations in the Netherlands comfortable and welcoming places. Their work has a direct influence on the work of their colleagues and thus an impact on the experience of the traveler, station visitor and tenant in the station area.

Defensie

Defensie is the Dutch army. It is a formal workplace and quite big.

IT roles

Officier Specialist Cyber.
They use their knowledge and skills about cyber for so-called offensive cyber capabilities in support of military operations. They are involved in testing vulnerabilities by hacking systems, securing their own systems by means of detection techniques and they are given the opportunity to develop software to support cyber actions. In short; a challenging job with many different facets from the cyber domain within a unique organization.

Officier ICT manager.
The liaison service (ICT) is the ICT company of the Army. The liaison department is responsible for all communication equipment and connections and ensures that the correct information is disseminated securely and on time. As an Officer ICT Manager they lead a platoon of the liaison service with approximately 50 soldiers. They are responsible for operational preparations, for the deployability of the platoon and equipment and for acting during exercises and operations. Both their men and the equipment must always be in perfect condition. As head of personnel affairs they therefore also monitor the training of their team, for example by preparing and providing training. As an Officer ICT Manager they will gain experience in all facets of ICT management: from hardware, software and networks, to personnel management and finance.

Officier Marechaussee Informatiemanagement (HBO/WO).
As a starting officer Information Management at the Royal Netherlands Marechaussee they work within the field of information management. The functions are located within the National Tactical Command (LTC) and the Education, Training and Knowledge Center of the Royal Netherlands Marechaussee (OTCKMar). The LTC takes care of the national management of the KMar operation on the basis of information obtained. Later they can progress to a position within your specialism at strategic level. During their entire career it is possible that they will be deployed for a certain period of time (weeks / months) during (international) missions or National Operations.

Matroos verbindingen / ict
As a sailor at the Operational Service Connections (ICT) they are the connecting link on the ship or ashore. They ensure that information reaches the right people on time. Together with their colleagues they manage the computer networks and the fully automated communication systems of the navy. They operate most transmitters, receivers and satellite communication systems remotely: from the bridge, the command center or the radio exchange. Communication is a broad concept for them. In addition to satellite connections, computer networks and radio connections, it also includes regular mail. And the messages they send can range from a personal letter or a simple e-mail to strategic information for headquarters during operations at sea.

Mederwerker ict
As an IT specialist they work at the Liaison Service of the Royal Netherlands Army. This component installs, operates and maintains all telecommunication equipment and connections. Think of telephone, radio, computers and satellite connections. The Liaison Service also provides mail delivery to the Royal Netherlands Army. Their work is very diverse. During a "normal" working day at the barracks, but also during exercises and broadcasts. One moment they are working on a radio connection in the middle of a snowy forest, and the next they are ensuring a good satellite connection in the heat of the desert.

Rijkswaterstaat

Rijkswaterstaat is a Dutch government company. They manage and develop national highways, waterways and waterways and are committed to a sustainable living environment. By protecting the land against flooding, Rijkswaterstaat ensures that everyone keeps their feet dry. They also ensure that there is sufficient and clean drinking water and that everyone can get from A to B smoothly and safely, both on the road and on the water. They are increasingly doing this together with other governments and market parties. They are a big company and their working culture is both formal and informal.

IT roles

Technisch adviseur vast onderhoud.
The technical advisor is part of the technology cluster in which the team members work geographically spread out from each other. The technical advisor provides substantive technical knowledge that is important in formulating the system, process and product tests towards market parties as part of System-Oriented Contract Management. This includes submitting risks, helping to determine the test plan and contributing to the implementation of the system, process and product tests.

Adviseur privacy en informatiebeveiliging.
The privacy and informationsecurity is responsible for the confidentiality and reliability of the information provision. They make sure no law are broken, the information is safe and the privacy is maintained.

Systeemarchitect / -integrator bruggen/sluizen industriële automatisering.
As a system architect they work on the construction and renovation of our tunnels and you contribute to the development and improvement of the corresponding standards. Together with the other system architects, they are thus the guardian of the quality of the dynamic systems of the tunnels to be constructed and renovated. They contribute to the development and assurance of the coherence of both the standards and the design of the tunnels to be constructed or modified in which these standards are applied. The results are lower costs for the realization of a new tunnel or renovation of an existing tunnel, a quality improvement in the field of reliable and more available infrastructure and on-time delivery and faster repair of the tunnel system.

Ict department head
The head of a small team of programmers. They don’t necessarily write code, but are social and the leader of their team. The contribute to their team with their leadership, knowledge and problem solving skills.

Data scientist
A lot of data that Rijkswaterstaat collects lands on the big data platform, such as sensor data of water levels. The data scientists can then perform analyses on that data. They work on finding patterns and automating the systems, which saves time and a lot of manual work.
