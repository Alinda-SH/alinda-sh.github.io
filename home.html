HBO-ICT

The HBO level suits me because ...

I didn't want to choose a study which was to theoretical. I have done atheneum, which gave me the oppertunity to go to university. I have visited the TU Delft, but I found myself wondering, do I want to have to study that much again. Aftwards I looked at some HBO and realised the HBO level suited me more.
I like the way HBO has more practical way of teaching, unlike university, which is more academic. It is more focussed on the near future, finding a job and give valuable experience.

The study programme suits my personality because ...

I have found out I like to code something. I had the subject Informatics and it turned out, I liked to code. Back then, I already knew I wanted to to something with technology. I visited the studies electrical engineering and mechanical engineering, and later the studies informatics and technical informatics, all HBOs.
To be honest, I hadn't planned to visit this study, I went to visit watermanagment and happend to stumble upon this study. In the end I chose this study.
This study programme suits my personality because the working method, agile, is focussed on feedback, learning from the feedback and striving to be better. I know it is impossible to be good at something immediately, you have to learn and sometimes you have to fail to become better. Agile working accepts that things can go wrong, but is focussed on improving.
